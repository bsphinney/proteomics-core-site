# Submissions — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/submissions

## Pre-Submission Requirements (3 steps)

### Step 1: Create a PPMS Account
New users must establish a PPMS account with a valid charge account prior to submitting samples.
- PPMS Login: https://ppms.us/ucdavis/login/?pf=8

### Step 2: Sample Labeling & Documentation
- Label samples on tube tops using "Initials-#" format without duplication
- Prepare an Excel spreadsheet mapping each labeled sample to its corresponding treatment or condition

### Step 3: Submit Forms
- A Sample Submission Form is mandatory for each sample set
- Include a printed copy with the shipment

## Initial Consultation
Contact facility personnel via the Contact Us Form before beginning service to discuss experimental details.
- Contact Form: https://proteomics.ucdavis.edu/contact/contact_us
