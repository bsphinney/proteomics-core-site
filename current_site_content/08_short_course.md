## Course Overview
Equal parts hands-on laboratory work, lectures and interactive data analysis. Introduces participants to mass spectrometry-based proteomics methodologies.

## Topics Covered
- Mass spectrometry and proteomics fundamentals
- Bottom-up quantitative nano-electrospray proteomics
- Sample preparation techniques
- Nano-scale HPLC and peptide-spectrum matching
- Quantitative proteomics using data independent analysis (DIA)
- Data analysis with Fragpipe, DIA-NN, and Spectronaut
