# Protein Interactions & BioID — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/protein-interactions-bioid

## Overview
Specializes in mapping protein neighborhoods through proximity labeling techniques. Captures weak interactions and transient cellular events beyond just strong, stable protein complexes.

## Key Services
- BioID/TurboID analysis
- Affinity purification mass spectrometry (AP-MS)
- Cross-linking mass spectrometry (XL-MS)

## How BioID Works (4-step process)
1. Express target proteins fused to promiscuous biotin ligase
2. Add biotin to create reactive labeling within ~10nm radius
3. Capture tagged proteins via streptavidin purification
4. Identify neighbors through mass spectrometry

TurboID offers faster labeling (10 minutes vs. 18-24 hours) for dynamic cellular snapshots.

## Data Analysis
- Contaminant filtering using internal background databases
- Visualization tools: dot plots and network maps (R or similar open-source software)

## Notable Research
Collaboration with the Deretic Lab revealed unconventional roles for ATG5 in retromer assembly and vesicular trafficking, published in eLife (2025).
