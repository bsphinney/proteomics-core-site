- Identifies over 3,000 proteins from plasma, serum, or CSF
- Enriches low-abundance biomarkers (kinases, cytokines)
- Species Agnostic: Validated for Human, Mouse, Rat, and more
- Cost-Effective: $210/sample

## Core Capabilities (4-card grid)
1. **Quantitative Discovery** — Global profiling for complex biological matrices. Ideal for comparative expression analysis across experimental conditions.
2. **Protein Interactions (PPI)** — Characterize protein complexes and interactomes using Proximity Labeling (BioID/TurboID) or classic IP-MS workflows.
3. **PTM Analysis** — Enrichment and characterization of post-translational modifications, including Phosphorylation, Ubiquitination, and Acetylation.
4. **Amino Acid Analysis** — Compositional analysis for pure proteins, peptides, and free amino acid quantification in physiological fluids.
