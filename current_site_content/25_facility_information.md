# Facility Information — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/facility-information

## Overview
Established in 2003 as a vital component of the UC Davis Genome Center. Applies cutting-edge technologies to examine genetic function in health and disease.

## Research Scope
Serves researchers across human and veterinary medicine, agricultural sciences, environmental health, and biomedical engineering.

## Collaborative Network
Interfaces with complementary service cores: bioinformatics, DNA and expression analysis, metabolomics, and yeast-based screening technologies.
