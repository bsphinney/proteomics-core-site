# Quantitative Protein Discovery & Biofluids — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/quantitative-protein-discovery-biofluids

## Overview
Data-Independent Acquisition (DIA) using advanced timsTOF and Orbitrap instruments for unbiased proteome analysis. Distinguishes itself from antibody-based panels through comprehensive protein detection.

## Key Advantages
- Species-agnostic methodology (works on mice, rats, crops, non-model organisms)
- Discovers thousands of proteins without bias, unlike targeted panels
- Cost-effective broad coverage alternative to large custom arrays

## Why Proteomics Over RNA-Sequencing
- mRNA levels typically explain only ~40% of the variability in protein levels
- **Temporal differences:** Proteins are more stable than mRNAs
- **Clinical advantage:** Proteins remain stable in archived samples (FFPE blocks), unlike degradable RNA
- **Buffering effect:** Cells actively maintain stable protein levels despite genetic variations

## Technical Infrastructure
- **Automated Processing:** Thermo KingFisher Apex robotic platform using magnetic bead technology
- **Scalability:** 96-well plate format ensures consistent processing across large cohorts
- **Analysis Support:** Visualizations, pathway analysis, and industry-standard software (Spectronaut, DIA-NN, FragPipe)
