# PTM Discovery — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/ptm-discovery

## Core Message
"PTMs dictate the functional state of the cell" by regulating signal transduction, gene expression, protein stability, and localization. Most modifications are sub-stoichiometric, requiring specialized enrichment strategies.

## Benchmarking Results (KingFisher SP3 automated workflow)
- Standard analysis: ~700 phospho-sites
- With enrichment (300ug input): 9,220 phospho-sites
- At 1ug input: ~2,900 proteins consistently identified

## Supported Modifications
1. **Phosphorylation** (Ser, Thr, Tyr, His) — uses TiO2 and Fe-NTA
2. **Ubiquitination** (Lys) — targets diglycyl-lysine remnants
3. **Glycosylation** (N-linked/O-linked) — HILIC and lectin-based strategies
4. **Additional:** Acetylation, methylation, oxidation, O-GlcNAc

## Critical Requirements
- Phosphatase/deubiquitinase inhibitors mandatory
- Minimum 200-300ug peptide recommended
- Starting protein: 600-700ug per sample
- Consultation recommended before sample collection
