# Submissions 2026 — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/submissions-2026

## Step 1: Account Creation
Users must establish a PPMS account with valid charge authorization before submitting forms.

## Step 2: Sample Preparation Guidelines

### Labeling Requirements
- Write Initials + Number (e.g., JD1) on tube tops
- Use sequential numbering for new sample sets
- Avoid tape or stickers on containers
- Match IDs to conditions in an Excel spreadsheet

### Tube & Shipping Specifications
- Use 0.5, 1.5, or 2.0ml snap-cap tubes (Eppendorf-style)
- Ship protein samples frozen on dry ice
- Transport bead-based samples on wet ice only
- Avoid screw caps, PCR tubes, or test tubes

## Step 3: Submission Form
Each sample set requires a completed form specifying organism, UniProt ID, buffer recipe, and biohazard rating. Include a printed copy in shipment.
