# Prices — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/prices

## Overview
"Almost all of the variability (in your results & in the pricing) is due to the protein extraction and sample prep."

## Price Estimates per Sample

| Service Type | UC Davis | Non-Profit | Industry |
|---|---|---|---|
| Protein | $270 | $333 | $360 |
| Peptide | $160 | $242 | $260 |

## Key Pricing Notes
- Base estimates include data analysis and 30-minute LC-MS runs
- Final costs depend on sample preparation type, actual LC-MS runtime, and additional labor
- Enrichment processes add $50 per sample
- LC-MS runs are charged per hour

## Cost Reduction Options
- Customers can perform protein digestion themselves and submit peptides to reduce costs
- High-throughput workflows available; contact facility for discussion
- Facility provides accessible protocols for independent sample preparation
