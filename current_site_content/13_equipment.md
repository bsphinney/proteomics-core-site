# Equipment 2026 — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/equipment-2026

## Bruker timsTOF HT
**Classification:** High-Throughput 4D-Proteomics Instrument
- Trapped Ion Mobility Spectrometry (TIMS) and PASEF technology
- Sequencing speeds up to 300 Hz for deep proteome coverage in short gradients
- Collisional Cross Section (CCS)-aware identification
- Optimized for large-scale plasma studies and biomarker discovery
- **Best for:** Clinical cohorts, high-throughput analyses, shorter gradient protocols

## Thermo Orbitrap Fusion Lumos
**Classification:** Tribrid Mass Spectrometer (Quadrupole + Linear Ion Trap + Orbitrap)
- SPS-MS3 technology for TMT quantification without ratio distortion
- Superior post-translational modification analysis (phosphorylation, glycosylation, crosslinking)
- Multiple fragmentation modes: CID, HCD, and ETD
- **Best for:** Complex samples, PTM studies, multiplexed quantification

## Thermo Orbitrap Exploris 480
**Classification:** Data-Independent Acquisition (DIA) Workhorse
- FAIMS Pro Interface for ion filtering
- Optimized for reproducible quantification across large sample cohorts
- Enhanced sensitivity through high-capacity ion transfer
- **Best for:** Routine quantitative studies, label-free analysis, robust performance

Users should consult with the Core Director for instrument selection.
