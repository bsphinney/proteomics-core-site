# Service Agreements — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/service-agreements-proteomics-core

## Agreement Process
- UC Davis requires use of their template agreement — no exceptions
- Institutions can sign the template as-is to begin work immediately
- Changes must be submitted as redlines on a separate page to gcbizoffice@ucdavis.edu

## Required Documentation
1. Proteomics Template Agreement Form (Google Doc)
2. Statement of Work (SOW) Cover Sheet specifying sample quantities and types

## Key FAQ
- Service agreements are **optional** unless the customer requests one
- Work quality remains unchanged with or without an agreement
- Typical processing time: about 2 weeks to 6 months
- Separate agreements needed for each Genome Center facility
- The core cannot sign customer-provided documents; all agreements route through the university's contracts team

## Processing Note
Facility cannot track agreement status during university business office review. Contact the Business Office administrative team for status updates.

## Contact
Agreement inquiries: gcbizoffice@ucdavis.edu
