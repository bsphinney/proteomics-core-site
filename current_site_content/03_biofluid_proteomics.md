# Biofluid Proteomics (Ceres Nanotrap) — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/biofluid-proteomics-using-ceres-nanotrap

## Technology Overview
Ceres Nanotrap uses hydrogel particles that "capture & concentrate" low-abundance proteins while excluding high-abundance noise like Albumin and Immunoglobulins.

## Performance Results
- Validation testing identified 3,176 Total Proteins
- Spans five orders of magnitude
- Enables detection of biomarkers in inflammation, oxidative stress, tissue damage, and cellular signaling pathways

## Cost Advantage
- ~$0.07 per protein vs competitor rates of ~$0.25 per protein for similar discovery-level analysis
- $210/sample

## Detected Biomarkers
- Over 140 kinases
- C-Reactive Protein
- Antioxidant enzymes
- Tissue leakage indicators
- Useful for liquid biopsy applications
