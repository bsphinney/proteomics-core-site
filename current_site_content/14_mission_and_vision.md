# Mission and Vision — proteomics.ucdavis.edu/mission-and-vision

## Background
Established in 2005 within the UC Davis Genome Center. Five scientists managing three mass spectrometers and three amino acid analyzers, with nearly two decades of LC-MS/MS expertise.

## Services
Protein identification, quantification, post-translational modification discovery, and targeted proteomics. One of the few core facilities in the world to offer Amino Acid Analysis and Edman Sequencing.

## Threefold Mission

1. **Service:** Deliver cutting-edge, affordable proteomic and protein characterization services with rapid turnaround time.
2. **Innovation:** Create and implement novel proteomics methods through collaborative customization for complex scientific projects.
3. **Education:** Teach proteomics through regular classes and a hands-on Summer Proteomics Short Course.

## Data Analysis
Comprehensive support beyond raw data delivery — various analysis levels using commercial software and custom scripts. Collaborates with the Bioinformatics Core to enhance data value.

## Educational Programs
- Summer Proteomics Short Course
- Virtual Proteomics Short Course (online)
- Data analysis video tutorials
