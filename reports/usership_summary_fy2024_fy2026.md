## 1. Facility Overview and Growth Trajectory

The UC Davis Proteomics Core Facility serves a broad and growing base of investigators spanning 320 unique PI groups over the three-year reporting period. The facility supports researchers from UC Davis, the wider UC system, other academic institutions nationwide, government agencies, and industry partners.

**Year-over-year retention:** 85 of 176 FY2024 PI groups returned in FY2025 (48%), and 59 of 166 FY2025 groups have already returned in FY2026 (36%, with 25% of the year remaining). Many investigators use the core on a project basis (1–2 submissions per year), so episodic rather than continuous engagement is the norm.

## 6. Summary

The UC Davis Proteomics Core Facility maintains a large, diverse, and growing user base:

- **320 unique PI groups** have utilized the facility over the past three fiscal years, drawn from 104+ distinct institutions.
- **User acquisition remains strong**, with 81 new PI groups in FY2025 and 63 new groups already in FY2026 (with 25% of the year remaining).
- **Revenue is well-diversified** across many investigators; no single PI group exceeds 5% of three-year revenue.
- **External reach is expanding**: non-Davis academic users now account for 35% of FY2026 revenue (up from 16% in FY2024), reflecting the facility's growing national reputation.
- **Annualized FY2026 revenue (~$863,016)** indicates stabilization after a FY2025 dip, with +2.1% growth projected over FY2025.

These data demonstrate sustained, broad-based demand for the proteomics core's services and support its continued operation and investment in next-generation instrumentation.
