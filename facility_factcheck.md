## CRITICAL: Equipment / Grant Mismatches

This is the most important finding. **The grant acknowledgments page associates the wrong instruments with the NIH grants.**

| Grant | NIH Reporter Says Was Purchased | Website Says | Status |
|-------|-------------------------------|-------------|--------|
| S10OD021801 ($600K, 2016) | **Orbitrap Fusion ETD** | Fusion Lumos | WRONG — different instruments |
| S10OD026918-01A1 ($600K, 2020) | **Q Exactive HF-X** | Exploris 480 | WRONG — different instruments |
| HHMI / Dr. Neil Hunter | Could not verify externally | timsTOF Pro 2 | Equipment page says "timsTOF HT" — which is it? |

**Brett: Did you upgrade/replace the original grant-funded instruments?** If so, the acknowledgments page should either:
- Reference the original instruments the grants purchased, OR
- Add language like "This grant funded the predecessor instrument (Orbitrap Fusion ETD) which has since been upgraded to the Fusion Lumos"

### Third S10 Grant Found (Not on Website!)
- **S10RR023642** ($491K, 2008) — funded an Applied Biosystems Q-trap 4000
- This grant is NOT listed on the acknowledgments page. Should it be?
